Let's get aquainted

Liam Gaffney is an Irish actor based in Dublin and Kildare. Liam began his acting journey at BowStreet Sceen Acting Academy and from there has learned from a handful of industry professionals through numerous workshops and courses.

Liam has most recently worked on season two of the Netflix series THE ALIENIST, ANGEL OF DARKNESS directed by David Caffrey and played one of the leads in TV pilot FLYBOYZ. Other recent credits include the award winning film THE SHIFT directed by Megan K. Fox. A Polish/Irish feature film I NEVER CRY with MK1 Productions, an award winning web series, DRAMA and the lead in the feature film THURSDAYS CHILD.

Liam has also done some commercial work as well as projects still currently in production that will come out in 2020. Liam is passionate about story telling and performance. He has accent training as well as stage combat training. Liam can also plays guitar and has training in MMA and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. Liam's credits can be found on both his Spotight account and his IMBD
